What is a truck weighbridge and how does a weighbridge system work?

A weighbridge, also known as a truck scale, is system of several components that work together to provide weight readings when a truck drives on to the scale. Comprised of a foundation, bridge or deck, load cell system, terminal, and any accessories. These parts work together to provide a weight that can be used for a monetary transaction, inventory tracking, and to check legal weight limits for off-road and over-the-road travel. Weighbridges/truck scales can range in size to accommodate the small farmer, to multi-national aggregate facilities.

How much does a weighbridge cost? How much does a truck weighbridge cost?

The cost of a weighbridge or weighbridge cost can vary depending on several variables including:

  3. material such as steel or concrete deck,
  4. load cell system purchased with the weighbridge,
  5. varying installation costs depending on the complexity of the installation,

What is the validity of weighbridge calibration certificate?

Calibration determines truck scale weighing performance. With routine scale calibration and adjustment, you can measure safely, ensure compliance and avoid the profit loss of inaccurate measurements. Achieve and maintain compliance through consistent procedures and adhere to local and global regulations and standards, such as NIST Handbook 44, OIML, and NMI.
